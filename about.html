Our Story

Born in Kenya,
Built for the World

Adpulse Industries was founded on a simple but powerful belief: that businesses across Kenya and East Africa deserve access to the same quality of digital solutions that global enterprises enjoy — at a price that makes sense.

We started as a small team of passionate engineers who saw a gap in the market. Too many businesses were either overpaying for foreign agencies that didn't understand the local market, or settling for generic, template-based work that failed to truly represent their brand.

Today, we are a full-service IT and software automation powerhouse — delivering custom websites, mobile apps, CRM systems, and end-to-end automation solutions that give our clients a measurable, lasting competitive edge.
